Welcome

Sawyer Solutions, LLC was founded on the concept of providing high quality, client oriented services to individuals, families, and organizations. For over 30 years, we have provided successful clinical services, organizational consultations, and training on a variety of issues including:


- Assessment, treatment, and management of sexual abuse,

- Working with at risk families, and

- System design to manage complex issues within organizations.


Our approach is successful because we take the time to understand what you need and then design collaborative solutions with your clear goals and expectations. We know we are successful because we work by refereral from those who have firsthand knowledge and confidence in our work.
